## What information we collect

Our guiding principle is to collect only what we need and that we will solely process this information to provide you with the service you signed up for.
We only share information with them that is required for the services offered, and we contractually bind them to keep any information we share with them as confidential and to process personal data only according to our instructions.

### As a visitor (no account)

As a visitor to the SimpleLocalize.io website:

- No personal information is collected.
- No information is sold or rent to third-parties.
- No information is shared with advertising companies.
- No information is shared with 3rd-parties other than specified in "Sub-processors".
- No information is mined and harvested for personal and behavioral trends.
- No information is monetized.

### As a user (with account)

- An **email address** is required to create an account. You need to provide us with your email address if you want to create a SimpleLocalize account. That’s just so you can log in and personalize your new account, and we can send you invoices, updates or other essential information.
- An **IP address** is processed and stored. That’s just so we can prevent service abuse and decrease the number of fake trial accounts when users provide us with different email addresses with the same IP address.
- The payment process is handled by a third-party payment provider If you choose to upgrade to a SimpleLocalize paid plan, the billing information and the **payment process is handled by Paddle**. See the Paddle Privacy Policy for full details.
- If you use social login (GitHub, Google Account, Microsoft Account), then we additionally process and store your **social login identifier**, **profile photo**, **first name**, **last name** (visible in your account settings as a name). You need to provide us with this information, so we can create your account or identify you once you sign in SimpleLocalize.io.
